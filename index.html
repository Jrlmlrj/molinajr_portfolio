Practicum Journey

During my practicum at DISTRIPHIL, we worked on developing two significant systems: the Return Upon Delivery (RUD) Form and the Product Arrival Report (PAR). The RUD Form system automates the process of returning products by customers, and the PAR system handles the documentation of products that have arrived. Both systems were initially manual, and our goal was to automate them to reduce the workload of warehouse staff, inbound controllers, and QA personnel, as well as to minimize errors that were common with handwritten forms.

Return Upon Delivery Form

Users will be given a code where they can access the system.

This module is responsible for adding returned stocks. Only Admins can input a product that is not registered yet in the database.

After adding stocks, all the stocks inputted will be compiled into a form sheet, which will automatically create a PDF and send it via email to designated staffs of the supply chain.

Product Arrival Report Form

PAR System interface for documenting product arrivals.

The PAR Stocks module manages the stock entries within the Product Arrival Report system, ensuring accurate documentation and updates of arriving products.
